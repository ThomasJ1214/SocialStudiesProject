About the Statue of David

The Statue of David is a masterpiece of Renaissance sculpture. Created between 1501 and 1504, it stands as a symbol of strength, beauty, and perfection.

Michelangelo's David represents the biblical hero just before his battle with Goliath, embodying human determination and grace.
